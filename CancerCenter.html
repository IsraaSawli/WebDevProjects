At the Children's Cancer Center of Lebanon (CCCL), we treat children with cancer totally for free from the age of 0 to 18. Being totally dependent on donations, and with a yearly budget of 15 million dollars,many kids are counting on big hearted people to help them become survivors. Together we can Save Lives and Celebrate Hope!

Provide support and resources for families going through cancer treatment in Virginia. Help, hope and hugs.
We work closely with key partners in the medical industry to provide the very best care possible to children.We work with a network of nine hospitals across Lebanon, giving
children access to the best facilities for optimal diagnosis and treatment.

The Children's Cancer Center of Lebanon (CCCL) is a non-profit medical institution dedicated to the treatment of paediatric cancer. The center is affiliated with St. Jude Children's Research Hospital in Memphis, Tennessee. Children's Cancer Center of Lebanon was established under Notice no. 138AD with the affiliation of St. Jude Children's Research Hospital.
